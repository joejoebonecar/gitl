Standard American Checkers Rules

  • Play on dark squares only.
  • Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square.
  • Captures are mandatory. Jump over adjacent opponent piece to empty square beyond.
  • Multiple jumps (chains) are allowed and must be continued if possible.
  • Reach the opposite end to promote to King. Kings move and capture in any diagonal direction.
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or leaving them with no legal moves.

This implementation enforces mandatory captures and multi-jump chains. AI is random but prefers captures.
